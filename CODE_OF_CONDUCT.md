# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment
We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and respectful environment for everyone.

## Expected behavior
- Be respectful and constructive in communication.
- Assume positive intent and focus on technical outcomes.
- Welcome feedback and collaboration.

## Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or abusive language.
- Doxxing, threats, or intimidation.
- Repeated disruptive behavior after warnings.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or ban participants who violate this code.

## Reporting
Report issues privately via the process in `SECURITY.md` or repository contact channels.
